Call for Papers

The International Conference on Biomedicine and Informatic (ICBI) provides a leading forum for disseminating the latest research in bioinformatics and health informatics. It brings together academic and industrial scientists from computer science, biology, chemistry, medicine, mathematics and statistics. We solicit high-quality original research papers (including significant work-in-progress) in any aspect of bioinformatics, genomics, and biomedicine. New computational techniques and methods and their application in life science and medical domains are especially encouraged. Relevant topics include but are not limited to:

Submissions

  • Submission Guidelines

    • 1. The ICBI encourages young scholars and master's and doctoral students to submit abstract papers. Innovative work related to bioinformatics that has been published or unpublished within the past year can be submitted in English, with a word limit of 1000 words;

    2. Please submit the abstract to jhu@nwpu.edu.cn and the submission deadline is July 15, 2024. Relevant experts will review the submitted manuscript, select oral special reports or wall posters for communication, and announce the results on July 20, 2024;

    3. The submission content includes: report title, author (please indicate work institution), abstract (in English, with word count limited to 1000 words), journal name (if published or accepted), report method (oral or wall poster), and contact information of the reporter.

    Notes:

    • If you need a visa invitation letter, please contact us in advance.
