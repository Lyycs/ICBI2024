Submissions

  • Submission Guidelines

    • 1. The ICBI encourages young scholars and master's and doctoral students to submit abstract papers. Innovative work related to bioinformatics that has been published or unpublished within the past year can be submitted in English, with a word limit of 1000 words;

    2. Please submit the abstract to jhu@nwpu.edu.cn and the submission deadline is XXXX. Experts will review the submitted manuscript, select oral special reports or wall posters for communication, and announce the results on XXXX;

    3. The submission content includes: report title, author (please indicate work institution), abstract (in English, with word count limited to 1000 words), journal name (if published or accepted), report method (oral or wall poster), and contact information of the reporter.
