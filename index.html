Introduction

In order to build a platform for academic exchange and achievement sharing in the field of bioinformatics, enhance the teaching and research capabilities of domestic scholars and researchers, deeply explore the research and application of new technologies and theories in brain neuroscience at home and abroad, analyze their mechanisms in the field of biomedical health, and promote the efficient layout of China's synthetic biology industry ecology, our university plans to hold the "International Conference on Biomedical and Informatics" in Xi'an, Shaanxi Province on August 1, 2024, using a combination of online and offline methods. The conference is planned to be hosted by Northwest Polytechnical University, with Associate Professor Hu Jialu serving as the chairman. The conference plans to invite relevant experts and scholars from home and abroad to jointly exchange the latest developments in the field of bioinformatics

The conference will focus on various omics data such as genomics, transcriptomics, and imaging, gathering major experts and scholars in the field of neurological diseases from abroad to jointly explore the cutting-edge dynamics, technological innovation, and industrial development of the industry, and explore the research and application of new technologies and theories in brain neuroscience at home and abroad.

The 1st Workshop on DMLS will be held with the 22nd IEEE International Conference on Data Mining on Dec. 1, 2022. Welcome researchers, educators, and Intelligent education enterprises to join this meeting.
