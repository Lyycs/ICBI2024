Introduction

To foster academic exchange and knowledge sharing in the realm of bioinformatics, and to bolster the teaching and research capabilities of domestic scholars and researchers, our university is organizing the "International Conference on Biomedical and Informatics" in Xi'an, Shaanxi Province on August 1, 2024, employing a hybrid format combining online and offline modalities. Northwest Polytechnical University is slated to host the conference, with Associate Professor Hu Jialu assuming the role of chairman. The conference aims to convene relevant experts and scholars from both domestic and international spheres to collectively delve into the latest advancements in bioinformatics.

With a focus on various omics data including genomics, transcriptomics, and imaging, the conference will serve as a platform for renowned experts and scholars in the field of neurological diseases to explore cutting-edge developments, technological innovations, and industrial advancements. Additionally, it aims to delve into the research and application of new technologies and theories in brain neuroscience, fostering collaboration and knowledge exchange among experts and scholars from around the world.
